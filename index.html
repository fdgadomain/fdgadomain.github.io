Fayetteville Disc Golf Association

Disc Golf Club serving all of Northwest Arkansas. Follow us on Facebook!

Scroll down to see what events we have in the area!

Monday Night Dubs at Northshore DGC

Perfect for the Beginner or Pro! Come out to Lake Fayetteville for a great Night of Disc Golf with Different Ages, Genders, and Personaltites! Random Partners will be assigned at the start and you will play 18 Holes of Golf! All in cost is $10. Contact Andrew Mcdonagh for more info.

Wacky Wednesday (Rotating Locations)

Follow the Facebook page for the Weekly Wacky Wednesday! Usually around 10-11 AM, This mini rotates locations around NWA and features a rotating format. 2 Off One, Doubles, One Disc Challenge and more! Usually has the option for skins, 5/5/5, and Normal Mini Entrance. Contact Ben Weber for more Info.

Wednesday Carter Park Mini in West Fork, AR

FDGA's will be partnering with the Disc Golf Dojo to host a mini Wednesday at Carter Park. They sometimes do doubles, singles, handicap league, and often play Basketball afterwards!

Creekside Thursdays in Farmington, AR

FDGA's newest mini hosted by Brady Gilleran at Creekside DGC! This will mainly be Thursdays and will include at least one PDGA League every summer. This is a low cost way to get extra PDGA rounds in to help out your rating! Typical cost is $10. Contact Brady Gilleran for more info.
